Data Source

This platform uses publicly available counselling data published by the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Madhya Pradesh. Historical opening and closing ranks from multiple counselling years are processed to generate predictions.

While every effort is made to ensure accuracy, results should be used as guidance only and not as an official admission guarantee.
